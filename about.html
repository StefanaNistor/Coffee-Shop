Hello weary traveler!

Come, stay a while, let's have a cup of coffee and reminisce about the old times; about how the first seed of this business was sowed... about our first struggles, our first sucess and what we've come to be now. Make yourself comfortable, I'll bring you some snacks- sweet or savory, whatever your liking is, for this tale may take some time to discuss.

The Great Idea

It all started one sunny morning whilst drinking a freshly brewed cup of coffee. A normal day, a normal week, a normal life. I've always loved coffee, ever since I first took my first sip. I saw it for the art that it was and always respected those who mastered it well- but how does one actually master such an art? Slowly, but surely I started investing into gaining knowledge about the craft of coffee making, and slowly I devled into other fun beverages. Friends appreciated it, family loved it and strangers were in awe. I realized I wanted to share my craft with the world not only so that I could find fellow coffee lovers, but also to create a safe space for people to come in and relax away from the problems of reality. This was, I realized then, what I was meant to do.

Bringing It To Life

To bring this idea to life I started hunting for a place to rent out. I wanted the location to be in an easily acessible spot and to be very beautiful. Then, I started investing into the best equipment I could find on the market to get the best results. I also invested in an accountant and a marketing specialist to help me get my bearings together. Afterwards, thing slowly started to settle in, the equipment was arriving, the raw materials were ordered and soon to be coming, the places was slowly being decorated tastefuly- but low and behold came my first failiure. One pipe broke whilst I was away and flooded my entire rented out space leading to diasaster. It took an extra month to regroup but I was invested into doing this! I knew it would be worth it!

Finding The Team

After finishing setting up the space I started to look for equally passionate people to help me on my mission. First, I wanted to obtain a contract with a cake shop to obtain the freshest sweets. I also had the same approach for the savory treats. Here I had the help of a friend's mother who was a passionate retired cook that still wanted to be working, and as such I hired her. Then, for serving personnel I made sure to choose only the best qualified people to ensure the best services when it comes to serving the beverages. Slowly, the team took shape and faster than I could blink, the business was up and running! Finally, sucess!

Slowly Blooming

Through hard work and continuous self improvement, our business slowly grew. We were very open to constructive criticism and tried out best to have a very customer friendly approach overall. We tried to have our menus be interactive and fun and to offer our customers the respite that they needed from the world, with the choice of fun minigames to receive discounts for their drinks. With lots of love and care we slowly started blooming and now we can say we are a true well-known brand that vows to offer you the best experience when it comes to consuming anything from our little shop. We welcome all with open arms and wish you the best of stays!
